

英语语法笔记:

1. 英语句式:
 1> 主语 + 谓语
	 eg. The universe remains
 2> 主语 + 系动词 + 表语
  	 eg. The food is delicious
 3> 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语
	 eg. He took his bag and left
 4> 主语 + 谓语 + 间宾 + 直宾
	 eg. Her father bought her a dictionary
 5> 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 + 宾补
	 eg. We made him our monitor
	

2. Be动词的形式:
be, is, am, are, was, were, being, been
 1> The man is back.
 2> They are back.
 3> He was back.
 4> They were back
 5> They have been back.  // 他们已经回来了, 动词完成时态
	
	be动词的用法

	后面接名词, 形容词, 地点副词或者短语作补足语
	也就是说, be动词,连接主语和补足语
	1> The man is a teacher.
	2> Mary's new dresses are colorful.
	3> My mother was in the kitchen.

	Be 动词的练习
	They re teachers		// 他们是老师
	He was a teacher before.  // 他以前是一名老师
	He has been a teacher for three years.  // 他已经当3年老师了
